Ohm's Law

Class 10

What's going on?

The battery pushes charge around the circuit — that push is voltage. The resistor makes it hard for charge to flow. The current is how much charge actually flows every second.

Double the voltage → double the current. Double the resistance → half the current. That simple proportion is Ohm's law.

Formula

V = I × R   →   I = V / R
