Magnetism & Motors

Class 10

What's going on?

Push a current through a wire and a magnetic field appears around it — that was Oersted's surprise. Spin a coil inside a magnetic field and a current appears — that was Faraday's. Electricity and magnetism are two faces of one thing.

A motor and a generator are the same machine run in opposite directions: the motor turns current into motion, the generator turns motion into current.

Where you see it

Many bicycles in India carry a small dynamo pressed against the wheel rim. Pedalling spins a magnet inside a coil — exactly your Generator scene — and the headlamp glows. Pedal faster: brighter lamp. Stop at a signal: the lamp goes dark.
